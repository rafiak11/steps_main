About Us



STEPS is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating equal opportunities for Indian students aspiring to study abroad. Our mission is to empower underrepresented students through personalized mentorship, workshops, and coaching—all offered free of cost. By focusing on the unique challenges faced by Indian Muslim women, first-generation and low-income students, and those from rural backgrounds, we help bridge the gap between their dreams and reality. Founded by Abdul Aleem and Rafia Khatoon, first-generation graduates with impressive academic achievements, STEPS is committed to making top-tier education accessible to all, regardless of their background.






Vision

STEPS envisions a world where every Indian student, regardless of their socio-economic background or geographical location, has the opportunity to study at the best universities globally. We aim to be a driving force in the movement toward educational equity, inspiring and empowering the next generation of leaders from underrepresented communities to make their mark on the world stage.






Mission

Our mission is to provide unwavering support to Indian students from underrepresented communities in their pursuit of higher education abroad. Through STEPS, we offer tailored assistance and resources that enable these students to overcome socioeconomic barriers and gain admission to the world's leading universities. We believe that every student, regardless of their background, deserves the opportunity to access a great education and achieve their full potential.

Our Services

Personalized Mentorship

Each student pairs with a mentor who excelled in top university admissions, guiding them to refine profiles, choose programs, and apply confidently.

10-Week Comprehensive Sessions

We offer workshops covering profile evaluation, test prep, university selection, resume/CV crafting, SOP writing, recommendations, scholarships, interview prep, and immigration support.

Our Team

Abdul Aleem

Rafia Khatoon
